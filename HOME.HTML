KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP SINCE 1992

KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP WAS STAABLIISHED IN THE YEAR1192 WITH THE DISOLACED COMMUNITY FROM THE SOUTH SUDAN DUE TO HEAVY WAR IN THE SOUTHERN PART OF THE COUNTRY, LOKI WAS THE MAIN RECIPTION AREA OF THE NEW ARRIVAL BEFOURE STABLISHMENT OF THE KAKUYMA REFUGEE CAMP IN THE YEAR 1992.
TODAY KAKUMA IS MADE UP OF DIFFERENT NATIONALITIES THAT FLEE FROM THEIR COUNTRY DUE TO SEVERAL REASONS. HERE ARE THE FOLLOWING NAITIONALITIES IN THE CAMP, SOUTH SUDANESE, SUDANESE,NIGERIAN, UGANDAN, CONGOLESE, ETHIOPIAN, RWANDESESE, BURUNDIAN AND SOMALIAN.
